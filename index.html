Home

Our College, the Govt. Polytechnic, Masabtank, the oldest polytechnics in the state, was established in 1923. Initially named as Osmania Central Technical College, it was located in the Mint Compound at Saifabad. In 1931 it was renamed as Osmania Technical College.

In 1947 our College was renamed as Government Polytechnic. In 1954 Technical Courses were introduced in Civil, Automobile and Mechanical Branches. Tele Communications (electronics) was introduced in 1957, Pharmacy in 1971 and Computers in 1986. The Masabtank Campus was inaugurated from the Academic year 1960-61.

ABOUT US

We, the alumni of this sacred place (you may call this institution a temple, a church, a mosque or with any divine name of your belief), our's is large network of people who have struggled during our college days and found success in our lives after long battles, We were few of those not-so-fortunate people, who had no proper guidance or mentors to help during our college days, We understand that like most of us, you would be undergoing the same hardships and because of this reason we have come together to be a part of your lives to guide you, mentor you and while we cherish and share our memories, build great moments with you.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to grow as a big community of successful professionals who will be responsible for the following:

  • Bring positive change in student's lives.
  • Be a source of information, source of energy, source of resources, source of inspiration and strength for students.
  • Ensure that the hardships of the students are reduced as much as possible by being their mentors, share the knowledge and experience that we gained over the years with students
  • Develop self-confidence in the students and guide them to succeed in their goals.
